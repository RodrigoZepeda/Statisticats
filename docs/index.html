Hola, soy Rodrigo Zepeda-Tello.

I’m a mathematician turned statistician. I’ve been modeling (with math… ehem) epidemics for the last 10 years. I have worked with differential equations (stochastic, ordinary, etc), abstract networks, bayesian statistics ~(and frequentist)~, random forests, neural networks, time series and survey designs. I also lecture a course in Applied Statistics and give short courses in R. In this blog I post stuff I find interesting (and course notes) oftentimes in English and sometimes in Spanish.

Feel free to contact me on Twitter, Github, and ResearchGate.

If you like my work consider:

  • hiring me to consult for your project,
  • commissioning a course,
  • buying me a coffee or a book,
  • subscribing to get the latest posts directly in your email.
